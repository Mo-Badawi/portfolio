01 — About

Trained to assume the software is guilty until proven innocent.

I'm a QA Engineer with a background in Management Information Systems, currently working across e-commerce content and inventory management on Adobe Magento Commerce. My day-to-day is a mix of catalog and content operations and the QA instinct to check whether the thing in front of me actually does what it claims to do.

I came into QA through structured training rather than accident: a dedicated Software Testing bootcamp, alongside Udacity nanodegrees spanning front-end development, UI/UX design, and data analysis. That combination is deliberate — I want to understand systems well enough to break them intelligently, not just click through a checklist.

I'm actively building toward broader software engineering skill — enough to read code, reason about architecture, and eventually contribute to it — while keeping test rigor as the core of how I work.
